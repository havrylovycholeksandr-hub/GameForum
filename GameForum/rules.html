Council Charter

Stormy Rimuru Community Guidelines

These rules mirror the standards you expect from forums and wikis everywhere. Follow them to keep the AnimeVerse vibrant, welcoming, and spoiler-safe for every pilot.

Respect everyone

No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks. Disagreements stay civil.

Stay on topic

Use the right categories, cite sources for wiki edits, and avoid spam or duplicate threads.

Mind spoilers

Tag major story beats, event reveals, and datamined content using spoiler formatting.

Zero tolerance policy

NSFW, illegal links, phishing, and cheating tools are banned and result in immediate suspension.

Posting checklist

  • Search before posting to prevent duplicates.
  • Credit artists and data sources when sharing media.
  • Flag problematic content instead of engaging.
  • Keep guides updated and mark outdated info clearly.

Moderation ladder

AnimeVerse Moderation follows a transparent escalation path. Each tier enforces the same community-first mindset.

Junior Moderator

Handles basic flag review, welcomes new pilots, and monitors spoilers.

Senior Moderator

Coordinates raids, escalates disputes, and curates community spotlights.

Junior Administrator

Oversees wiki structure, event hubs, and advanced tooling rollouts.

Senior Administrator

Sets policy, approves major updates, and liaises with the council.

Reporting

If you spot a violation, use the in-app report button, ping a moderator in chat, or email moderation@animeverse.gg.

Abuse of the report tool is itself a rule violation.

By continuing to use AnimeVerse Clash services you agree to these guidelines.
